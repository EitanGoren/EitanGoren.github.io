Shopik

The Tinder of Fashion



  • Category : Android App Development
  • Language : Java
  • Release date : 01 September, 2020
  • Development Time : 4 Months
  • About the App :
    E-commerce Application for Android.
    Explore thousands of fashion items and save your favorite ones. The items are real fashion items with up to date data, like prices, discounts & images.
    After each swipe right of an item, the algorithem will update all items match percentage for you. You can sort items by match, prices, sale, brand etc..
